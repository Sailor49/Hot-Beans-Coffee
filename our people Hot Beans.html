Meet Our People

At Hot Beans Coffee, it’s the people who make the magic happen!

Our Story

Founded by passionate coffee lovers, Hot Beans Coffee isn't just a coffee shop—it’s a community. Behind every cup is a dedicated team of baristas, roasters, and dreamers who believe in delivering more than just caffeine. We brew warmth, connection, and excellence.

Our Team

Sophia Bennett

Founder & CEO

Sophia’s love for coffee started during her backpacking trips across Central America. She created Hot Beans Coffee to bring ethically sourced beans to every neighborhood. Her vision? Coffee that connects people globally.

Lucas Martin

Master Roaster

Lucas has been roasting coffee for over 15 years. He’s the genius behind our signature blends, meticulously crafting each roast to ensure the perfect balance of flavor and aroma.

Mia Tran

Head Barista

Mia’s latte art is Instagram-famous, but her passion lies in teaching the next generation of baristas. She leads our in-house barista training program with infectious energy.

Ethan Gomez

Sustainability Lead

Ethan ensures that every bean we source and every cup we serve is sustainable. He partners with farmers to build long-term relationships and advocate for fair trade practices.

Emma White

Customer Experience Manager

Emma’s goal is simple: to make every customer smile. From designing cozy café spaces to refining our app experience, she makes sure you feel at home with Hot Beans Coffee.
